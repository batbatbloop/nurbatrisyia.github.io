Hidden Beauty: Pang Sua Pond

Pang Sua Pond at Bukit Panjang

If you're not from the North-West area of Singapore, I bet you did not know about the small but charming Pang Sua Pond. Don't underestimate its beauty because of its small size. Like a little chilli padi, it can surprise you how much you can see and do there. It is stunning any time of the day with its newly developed boardwalk and floating wetlands. The best part is that it is conveniently located near many other ameneities (so you don't have to worry about toilets).
